Revisiting HiLo

"HiLo", is the fourth album released by Jack Stauber on April 14, 2018. The album consists of a mixture of songs with a genre and feeling of pop, funk, and a little bit of of rock. The album cover is of a keyboard with the album name HiLo, and Jack Stauber's name on it. It was released on Apple Music, Spotify, and Bandcamp.

About Jack

Jack Stauber is a singer, songwriter, and animator from McKean, Pennsylvania.

Jack's music genre is often described as avant-garde, or indie alternative. His music is a blend of pop, electronic, and folk. Jack's YouTube channel features a multitude of different videos, which typically include MS-paint animation, 3D animation, stop-motion animation, and other mixed media. Nearly all of his content (especially since 2016) is recorded onto a vhs tape, giving it an old-timey aesthetic. Themes within the videos may be that of a typical song or something more cryptic in nature. He also makes animated cartoons/shorts on his channel usually made with Microsoft Paint, but more recently have been appearing in different or more unique art-styles. He has also gained the attention of Adult Swim, and has aired one of his videos on an episode of "Off the Air", made an "adult swim smalls", created a whole mini-series called "SHOP: A Pop Opera", and then made a short film called OPAL.
